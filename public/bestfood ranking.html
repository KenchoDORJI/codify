Best Foods in Bhutan

Top 3 must try food recommended by Kencho

SIKAM PAA

The wind dried pork will come in 8-10 cm long and potatoes or radish and spicy red chillis are added as well.

EMA DATSHI

A cheesy chilli curruy is a national dish of Bhutan and must try once if you have been to Bhutan

KEWA DATSHI

It's a curry made up of fine chopped potatoes with cheese.
